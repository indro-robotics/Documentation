Hunter is an AgileX Robot designed for Low-speed Autonomous Driving scenarios. It leverages front-wheel ackermann steering and rocker suspension to cope with obstacles along its way. The new-generation power system offers better payload capacity and longer endurance. The revolutionary design of battery bin enables a quick-change process. It comes with the CAN232 interface for secondary development.

Visit AgileX for more information on the Hunter Robot.
